Signature Industrial and IT Plots - City of Colours

Sector 37D, Gurugram | A New Era of Industrial Development

Welcome to Signature Industrial and IT Plots - City of Colours, a landmark development in Sector 37D, Gurugram, designed to redefine industrial and IT infrastructure. Spread across prime real estate, this project offers premium industrial and IT plots ranging from 112.1 to 1000 sq. yards, providing the perfect canvas for your business aspirations.

Strategically located with excellent connectivity to major business hubs and transportation networks, City of Colours is designed to meet the evolving needs of modern industries. The development features wide roads, ample green spaces, and state-of-the-art infrastructure, ensuring a sustainable and efficient working environment for all businesses.

With pre-approved building plans and all necessary clearances, City of Colours offers a hassle-free investment opportunity. The project is designed to accommodate a variety of industrial and IT/ITeS businesses, making it an ideal destination for entrepreneurs and corporations looking to establish or expand their operations in the National Capital Region.

About Signature Global

Building homes for modern India

Signature Global is the Largest real estate development company in Delhi-NCR, focussed on affordable and mid segment housing (LIG and MIG) in terms of unit supplied (in the below Rs.80 lakh price category) between 2019 and 2021 with a market share of 19%.

The company commenced its operations in 2014 through its subsidiary, Signature Global Private Limited, with the launch of our Solera project on 6.13 acres of land in Gurugram, Haryana. We have grown our operations over the years and in less than a decade, and as of March 31, 2022, we had sold 23,453 residential and commercial units, all within the Delhi NCR region, with an aggregate Saleable Area of 14.59 million square feet.
