Where The Sky Unfolds A Life Without Limits

Signature DeLuxe DXP and Signature Titanium SPR were huge successes, and now Signature Global is thrilled to announce the opening of Twin Towers in Sector 84, New Gurgaon. In the soon-to-be Global City, Signature Global Twin Towers DXP Sector 84 Gurgaon is ideally located close to the Dwarka Expressway. The project’s most notable feature is its ideal location, which puts it in close proximity to important social infrastructure like colleges, hospitals, schools, and shopping centers.

The major highlight of this project is The Sky Deck, which gives you mesmerizing and breathing views from the top of the tower. It will be an excellent location to enjoy the evening or morning with your friends and family. The apartments have a unique and spacious design, with a 3.6m floor-to-floor height.

About Signature Global

Building homes for modern India

Signature Global is the Largest real estate development company in Delhi-NCR, focussed on affordable and mid segment housing (LIG and MIG) in terms of unit supplied (in the below Rs.80 lakh price category) between 2019 and 2021 with a market share of 19%.

The company commenced its operations in 2014 through its subsidiary, Signature Global Private Limited, with the launch of our Solera project on 6.13 acres of land in Gurugram, Haryana. We have grown our operations over the years and in less than a decade, and as of March 31, 2022, we had sold 23,453 residential and commercial units, all within the Delhi NCR region, with an aggregate Saleable Area of 14.59 million square feet.
